About Us

We are a diversified, global team with a philosophy of kindness in our business actions and engagements. With operations and direct investments in Sri Lanka, Australia, Malaysia, Dubai and United Kingdom, our diverse leadership brings together a band of brothers of different cultural backgrounds who leverage their humanity to manage our asset base, grow shareholder value and to give back to our community.

Innovation

As key guiding principles, innovation is at the very heart of Meritum Global, from providing a workplace environment that nurtures new ideas and creative thinking, to forging the way from a knowledge-based to a sustainable, innovation-driven economy through our multi-industry companies.

Excellence

The Meritum Global Management Framework ™ has been developed to create an environment to encourage proactive innovation, unlock untapped opportunities and potential value. Merit

One earns merits through postive actions and by giving back to community. We strive to uphold the expectations and standards to the highest degree and cultivate a team who are dependable, reliable, honest when dealing with people from all corners of the globe.

Loyalty

We achieve our goals through mutual commitment and respect with each and every member called upon to contribute to our common goals. We firmly believe that the workplace is the ideal starting point to living responsibly, adopting a healthier lifestyle, whilst providing an environment where creativity and innovation can thrive.

Integrity

We are committed to innovative sustainability practices focused on building a better world for future generations. We are changing the way we do business to preserve natural resources and the environment by reducing waste and reusing or recycling production materials, when possible. We lead by example and invest as needed to help change the world today.

People

We proactively encourage responsible living through healthy lifestyles, entrepreneurial thinking, contribution to the community, whilst caring for the environment around us. As a responsible corporate citizen Meritum Global value the wellbeing of our employees, by encouraging creative and innovative thinking, the future community and the environment, which form the four core pillars of our Corporate Responsibility.

What We Do

Through management of a diverse portfolio of assets, Meritum Global is supporting the development of a sustainable, innovation-led conglomerate that leverages diverse experience and global scale to drive ecologically sustainable profitable businesses that deliver on commitment to the triple bottle line of People, Planet and Profit all being of equal importance.

Investment & Finance

We actively strive for shared value - rather than focusing solely on shareholder value - and believe in protecting and enhancing the earth and its people, rather than exploiting these resources for financial gain.

Stewardship & Engagement

By focusing on long-term sustainable effectiveness rather than short-term financial outcomes we offer a unique approach to corporate governance. Our core economic principles - people, profit, planet - are equally weighted to create a world with healthy employees, communities, and natural systems, rather than engaging in sustainability initiatives purely to offset poor business practices.

Corporate Advocacy

Taking a leadership roles in solving the problems of the world, rather than assuming that is the role of governments, and that businesses need only comply with what it is legislated to do. Our goal is to provide a standard of excellence that other organisations envy, and a team that genuinely feel part of the solution.

Wealth Distribution

We collaborate with our global partners, stakeholders and sectors to build a global system - governed by our core economic different principles- to deliver a new sense of equity and justice, rather than wielding corporate power to steer a disproportionate share of wealth to capital. Our success is ultimately your success.
